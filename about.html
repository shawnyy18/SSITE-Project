History

SSITE was established in 2021 as an dedicated organization to supporting IT students. Despite being relatively new, SSITE has consistently aimed to provide outstanding support and engaging activities for IT students. The organization has quickly become a valuable resource for students, offering a range of services from academic assistance to leisure activities.

Mission

The mission of SSITE is to offer comprehensive assistance to students, addressing their academic and personal needs. We strive to provide engaging activities and foster active connections among students, ensuring they have access to the resources and support necessary for both their educational and recreational pursuits.

Vision

SSITE envisions a closely-knit community where students can enjoy meaningful interactions and experiences. Our goal is to contribute to the success of our members by offering continuous support and opportunities for personal and academic growth. We aim to see our students thrive and achieve their full potential with the help of SSITE.
